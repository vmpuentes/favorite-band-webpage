High Pulp is a band based in Seattle, Wa. They started in January of 2017 and have held a residency at the Royal Room every wednesday at 10pm and have named their residency "Funk Church". High Pulp plays "dance music that you can chew on!", a mix of Funk,jazz,punk, and improvisation.
